59 REAL ESTATE PORTFOLIO IMPORT

ADMINISTRATOR ONLY
Portfolio import, replace portfolio, backup restore and Firebase migration are restricted to the Administrator. Property Managers do not see or trigger these controls.

Use one row per unit.

Required columns:
property_code, property_name, unit_number, unit_status, monthly_rent

Allowed unit_status values:
occupied, booked, vacant, inspection, maintenance, renovation, unavailable

For occupied or booked units:
tenant_name, contract_start and contract_end are required.
Use YYYY-MM-DD for dates.

Payment schedule fields:
- first_due_date: normally the lease start date.
- grace_period_days: optional number of days before an installment becomes overdue.
- expected_payment_method: Post-Dated Cheque, Cash, Card, Bank Transfer, Auto-Debit, Replacement Cheque or Other.

IMPORTANT PAYMENT RULE
Contractual due dates are generated from the lease terms and remain fixed. The actual paid date only records whether an installment was early, on time or late; it never shifts the next due date.

For vacant, inspection, maintenance, renovation or unavailable units:
leave tenant and contract fields blank.

Do not rename column headers.
Property code + unit number must be unique.
Enter amounts as numbers only, without QAR or commas.

The importer separates each flat row into Property, Unit, Kahramaa, Tenant, Contract and Payment Schedule records.
In production, the same validated importer will write through the Firebase provider.
