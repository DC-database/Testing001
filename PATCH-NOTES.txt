59 REAL ESTATE - PATCH v0.8.8
Base version: v0.8.7
Patch name: Bilingual Tenancy Contract Generator

PURPOSE
This patch converts the existing simple contract printout into a controlled bilingual tenancy contract workspace based on the supplied four-page company contract format.

MAIN FEATURES
1. English-left / Arabic-right contract layout.
2. Four fixed A4 pages matching the supplied contract structure:
   - Page 1: Parties and clauses 1-3
   - Page 2: Clauses 4-11
   - Page 3: Clauses 12-18
   - Page 4: Clauses 19-22 and signature boxes
3. Actual 59 Real Estate SVG logo in the contract header.
4. Automatic data from the active property, unit, tenant and lease:
   - Tenant name, QID/CR, mobile and email
   - Property and unit information
   - Electricity and water numbers
   - Rent, security deposit, dates and notice period
   - Payment method and maintenance contacts
5. Editable Contract Details and paired English/Arabic clauses.
6. Draft watermark on unapproved contracts.
7. Save Draft, Preview/PDF, Approve & Lock and Administrator Reopen workflow.
8. Approved contract snapshots remain unchanged when the master template is edited later.
9. Administrator master template editor under Settings > Data Management.
10. Contract changes are recorded in the audit history.
11. Desktop print and mobile PDF/share-ready document preview.

ROLE BEHAVIOUR IN THE CURRENT DEMO
- Property Manager: edit and save contract drafts.
- CEO: review and approve/lock a contract.
- Administrator: edit drafts, approve, reopen final contracts and edit the master template.

PRODUCTION FIREBASE NOTE
The new contract document is stored inside its related contract record. When Firebase becomes the live provider, the same data-service functions should write the document snapshot to Firebase and Firebase Security Rules must enforce the role workflow.

LEGAL NOTE
The bilingual wording is prepared from the supplied company contract and cleaned for system use. The final English and Arabic legal text must be reviewed and approved by the company's authorized legal adviser before production signing.
